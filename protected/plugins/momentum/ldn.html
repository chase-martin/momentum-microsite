why attend

Lead the charge, keepit digital

Although businesses have saved millions of dollars by switching processes from analog to digital, their journey to becoming 100% digital has only just begun. MOMENTUM 2014 will gather industry luminaries and forward thinkers from around the globe for a fast-paced and entertaining exchange of ideas about how to expand and integrate digital processes. Between keynotes, breakouts, demos and conversations, you’ll bring home actionable strategies to help shrink costs, conserve resources and make your company truly unstoppable.

A day of discovery

London Mayor Boris Johnson once said: 'London is a city where around almost every corner there has been a great Eureka moment.” Given the Londoner’s affinity for beer, no doubt numerous of those “aha” moments struck at 52 Chiswell Street – the site of the former Whitehead Brewery. Entertaining Londoners since 1750, The Brewery is a premier event venue near the ‘Silicon Roundabout’ at Old Street and the creative community of Shoreditch.
